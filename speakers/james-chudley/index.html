James Chudley

User Experience Director at cxpartners

James Chudley

User Experience Director
cxpartners
London, UK

James has worked in the field of UX for 14 years within academia and both the public and private sectors. He is a User Experience Director at cxpartners where he leads UCD projects for the great and good of the web. He collaborated with the biggest clients around the world – Nokia Erikson and Expedia, among others.

James wrote ‘Smashing UX Design’ and ‘Usability of Web Photos’ and co-founded UXBristol in 2011. He loves all things photographic and combines his love of photography and UX at his blog www.photoUX.co.uk.

It is a great pleasure to welcome him at UX Lausanne since we love his subtle perspective on what UX leadership means and his pragmatic approach… furthermore, he rocks the stage.

Keynote:
Essential UX leadership skills

Friday | 13:50 – 14:35

What skills do you need to ensure smooth projects and effective outcomes?

UX projects often present us with all sorts of leadership and management challenges – from problems with people, expectations, changes in requirements and challenging design problems.

Strong leaders are critical to great design projects and great products – but what do they actually do that makes them so effective? How do they ensure that projects run smoothly and have effective outcomes? How do they create great working relationships that make the experience of working on the project as enjoyable as the end result?

What you will learn

In this talk, you will learn soft skills you need to improve your own leadership skills that will make a huge positive impact on the success of your own UX projects.

Who is this talk for

Everyone interested in UX management.
